Recommended Accommodations

We do not have any discount with any accommodation. Conference participants should book their accommodation by themselves. As there are only very few accommodations near the conference venue, we do recommend participants to consider accommodations in the Surfers Paradise area. From Surfers Paradise, one can easily take Glink Tram GCR1 to the venue in 30 minutes. Our reception and banquet will be also taken place in a restaurant at Surfers Paradise!

Travel from Gold Coast Airport to Surfers Paradise

Option Details Duration Cost
Bus and Tram Route 777/700: Take the TransLink bus 777 or 700 from Gold Coast Airport to Broadbeach South Station.
Tram: From Broadbeach South Station, transfer to the Glink tram towards Helensvale and get off at Surfers Paradise. 		Approximately 1 hour Around AUD 6-10
Shuttle Service Airport Shuttle: Companies like Con-X-ion offer shuttle services that can take you directly from the airport to your accommodation in Surfers Paradise. Approximately 30 to 45 minutes Around AUD 20-30 per person
Taxi or Ride-Sharing Taxi: Taxis are available outside the airport terminals and provide direct service to Surfers Paradise.
Ride-Sharing: Services like Uber or DiDi are available for pick-up at the airport. 		Approximately 30 to 40 minutes, depending on traffic Around AUD 80 for a taxi; ride-sharing costs can vary
Car Hire Rent a Car: You can hire a car from the airport and drive to Surfers Paradise. Approximately 30 to 40 minutes Varies by car rental company and type of vehicle

Travel from Brisbane Airport to Surfers Paradise

Option Details Duration Cost
Train and Tram Train: Take the Airtrain from Brisbane Airport to Helensvale Station. The train departs every 15 minutes during peak times and every 30 minutes during off-peak times.
Tram: From Helensvale Station, transfer to the Glink Tram towards Broadbeach South and get off at the Surfers Paradise stop. 		Approximately 2 hours Around AUD 30 for adults
Bus Direct Bus: Greyhound Australia operates a direct bus service from Brisbane Airport to Surfers Paradise. Check their schedule for departure times. Approximately 1.5 to 2 hours, depending on traffic Around AUD 25-30
Taxi or Ride-Sharing Taxi: Available outside the airport terminals. A taxi ride can be more expensive but offers convenience.
Ride-Sharing: Services like Uber or DiDi are available from the airport. 		Approximately 1 to 1.5 hours, depending on traffic Around AUD 200 for a taxi, ride-sharing costs can vary
Car Hire Rent a Car: You can hire a car from the airport and drive to Surfers Paradise. Approximately 1 to 1.5 hours, depending on traffic Varies by car rental company and type of vehicle
